Built out of frustration with spreadsheets and bloated ATS tools during recruiting season. Ponovo is a focused job tracker that gets out of the way and lets you manage your pipeline with clarity.

## What it does

Track applications across every stage — from applied to offer — with a kanban-style board and a timeline view per company. Each application stores the job description, notes, contacts, and a full history of status changes.

## Under the hood

The stack centers on **Drizzle ORM** over a **Neon PostgreSQL** database, with **React Query** for optimistic updates that make the UI feel instant. Auth is handled by **Clerk**, which gave me SSO and session management without touching user infrastructure.

The frontend uses the Next.js App Router with a deliberate RSC/client boundary split — data fetching on the server, mutations through Server Actions, client state only where necessary.
